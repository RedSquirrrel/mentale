Lockdown Mental Health Assistance

What is our mental state?

We are all in a mental struggle right now, you're not alone. As lockdowns started, depression rates rised by 37%, which is a high risk by itself on humans. We all have more time to think about what we're doing and time is passing away quicker, it seems to most. We all try to pass time by playing a game, as we're stuck at home, play with friends or be with our families. But is this the only thing we can do?

What else can we do?

Watch this video of motivation, to combat depression and see ways around it. Try to achieve a goal, try exploring new hobbies and ways to keep yourself at work. Try exercising. Go on YouTube and look up a 10-30m exercising video. It can be cardio, or whatever you want. Try to learn something new, you can maybe find a hobby which could be a job eventually. We can spend our time wisely in the time we're at home.

By having motivation, you have a goal. Reaching it gives you happiness and pain.
