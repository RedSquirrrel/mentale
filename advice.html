What to do next?

As you can see, we are all in a similar situation. But it's always up to us if we see the good side or bad side of things. We can waste our time by looking at how horrible Covid19 is and how terrible it is staying inside all the time, or work through our struggles and find our motivation, whether it is a hobby, a person, a desire. Whatever your motivation might be, you will need to work for it and no one else will work for you to achieve it. Certain people will help you, such as relatives and friends, but they can't do it for you. You will smile at your past once you reach your goal.

By having motivation, you have a goal. Reaching it gives you happiness and pain.
